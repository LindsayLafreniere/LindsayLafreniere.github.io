Jul 2023 - Jul 2024 · 1 yr 1 moJul 2023 to Jul 2024 · 1 yr 1 mo Producer of two technology-focused branded podcasts -- The Catalyst by Softchoice and Innovation Heroes by SHI International.

I produced the podcast Talk it Forward hosted by the Vancouver Board of Parks and Recreation, with support from the City of Vancouver.

Talk it Forward is a podcast that uses the power of dialogue to advance equity and diversity in the workplace. The season explores the challenges women and gender-diverse people continue to face in their careers and in pursuing leadership roles.
